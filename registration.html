Sparkstone Trading Challenge
sponsored by



The Sparkstone Trading Challenge provides a chance for Cornell students to learn about algorithmic trading and gain real-world experience building strategies on our proprietary analytics platform. The top strategies will win prizes and have a chance to have dinner with our headlining sponsors: Flowtraders! Everyone who participates will have their resume sent to our sponsors in a resume book.
